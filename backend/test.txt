🍗 Garlic Butter Chicken Thighs

Ingredients
	•	6 bone-in, skin-on chicken thighs
	•	2 tbsp olive oil
	•	4 tbsp unsalted butter
	•	5 garlic cloves, minced
	•	1 tsp paprika
	•	½ tsp dried thyme
	•	½ tsp dried rosemary
	•	½ tsp salt (adjust to taste)
	•	½ tsp black pepper
	•	½ cup chicken broth
	•	2 tbsp chopped fresh parsley (for garnish)

Instructions
	1.	Pat chicken thighs dry with paper towels. Season both sides with paprika, salt, and pepper.
	2.	Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Sear chicken, skin side down first, until golden brown and crisp (about 7–8 minutes per side). Transfer to a plate.
	3.	Reduce heat to medium, melt butter in the same skillet, and add garlic, thyme, and rosemary. Sauté until fragrant (about 1 minute).
	4.	Pour in chicken broth, scraping up any browned bits.
	5.	Return chicken to skillet, skin side up. Spoon some garlic butter sauce over the top.
	6.	Cover and let simmer for 15–20 minutes until chicken is fully cooked (internal temp 165°F / 74°C).
	7.	Garnish with fresh parsley and serve with rice, potatoes, or roasted veggies.
